Who We Are

Music in Service of Community

VoiceGivers is a professional chamber choir founded in Pittsburgh in 2019. Under the direction of Artistic Director Joseph Stuligross, the VoiceGivers Choir provides audiences with exceptional performances of unique and diverse music, blending pieces by living composers with many centuries of choral repertoire.

In partnership with nonprofit organizations, VoiceGivers presents choral performances that are intended to engage and inspire. Programs integrate music with personal narrative stories, multimedia, poetry, and other forms of expression to engage audiences in the emotions and reality of important issues facing vulnerable members of the community.

Proceeds from performances are donated to the nonprofit partner.

Music

Exceptional performances blending contemporary composers with centuries of choral tradition, all under the direction of Joseph Stuligross.

Inspiration

Each program spotlights a community need, pairing musical selections with the stories of nonprofit partners addressing those issues.

Connection

We bring performers and audiences into direct contact with the change-makers working to transform our community.

"How can beautiful music produced by fantastic singers be directed toward the betterment of our world?"

The founding question of VoiceGivers
