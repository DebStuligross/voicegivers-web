Help and Hope for Vulnerable Children

Holy Family Institute  ·  Pittsburgh, PA

VoiceGivers presented its second benefit concert in partnership with the Holy Family Institute. The program brought together powerful choral music with the mission of an organization dedicated to strengthening families and serving vulnerable children in the Pittsburgh region.

2019

Giving Help and Hope to Vulnerable Children

Fox Chapel Presbyterian Church  ·  Pittsburgh, PA

The inaugural VoiceGivers concert, part of the Fox Chapel Presbyterian Church Special Music Series, was a resounding success. More than 100 audience members attended, and the concert raised over $2,000 for the Pediatric Palliative Care Coalition — helping advance its mission to improve quality of life for children with serious illness and their families.

The program featured "You Do Not Walk Alone" by Dominick DiOrio, conducted by Joseph Stuligross.

Benefiting: Pediatric Palliative Care Coalition
